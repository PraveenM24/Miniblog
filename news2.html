INAGURATION OF CSE DEPT


Memories are enduring treasure of the heart




Tpgit Alumini meet was held on 17,january in Tpgit campus,Vellore. Close to 100 Tpgians, who had graduated from the institute at diffrent times in last 25 years, and currently in industryand been a entrepreneur, converged at Auditorium, Tpgit. Joining with students, faculty and staff they spent several hours together in a charged atmosphere.

The chief guest of the event was Mr.Sivaguru, IAS, subcollector of kodaikanal. Mr.SIvaguru is also TPGIT's alumini association member. Gracing the occassion by his presence, he shared his time and thoughts with those assembled. A new alumini and student interaction website "TPGIT miniblog" was launched by him.
